PhotoMarks for Mac

PhotoMarks is a full-featured solution for visually watermarking your copyrighted images in batch mode. A lightweight, purpose-built tool that outperforms general photo editors for this essential task.

Batch Processing

Process hundreds of images at once with multi-core processor support. Efficient, fast, and reliable for large workloads.

Custom Text Watermarks

Pixel-perfect positioning, tiling, rotation, multiline text, custom textured fonts, shadows, strokes, and bounding boxes. Full control over every detail.

Logo Watermarks

Precise placement, scaling, rotation, tiling, strokes, and shadows. Protect your brand effortlessly across any image.

Smart Filters

Auto-resize distinguishing landscape from portrait, auto-rotate for correct orientation, customizable rename with keywords, and graphical frames with grunge, vintage, or border effects.
